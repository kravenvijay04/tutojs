Wings of fire

A.P.J. Abdul kalam

"Wings of Fire" is Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam's autobiography, chronicling his journey from a humble background to becoming India's President. It inspires with tales of perseverance, highlighting his contributions to science and space exploration. Through his narrative, Kalam emphasizes the value of hard work and determination in achieving success. The book stands as a testament to the power of dreams and resilience.
